Este Visser

I love listening to music, and Este's arrangements bring my compositions to life. She is the best arranger in Glasgow and has had works performed by the Scottish Chamber Orchestra, BBC Scottish Symphony Orchestra, Capella String Quartet, James Grant, Chris Thomson to name a few. Her arrangements have been heard on BBC televison and at the Celtic Connections with Rachel Sermanni, and Karen Matheson, of Caipercaillie. Her arrangements have been recorded by ClockworkSessions
