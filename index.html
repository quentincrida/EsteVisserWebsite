Esté Visser

Welcome to my site! I am Esté Esté Visser, and I am an arranger and pianist.

Having years of experience playing in orchestras and chamber music on the violin, as well as accompanying singers and choirs at the piano, I have a wealth of diverse musical experience. I love listening to other peoples' music and writing out their arrangements for them, giving their works a new lease of life. So, if you are in a band looking for an arranger, or a string quartet or trio looking for arrangements and transcriptions, I can certainly help you.
Based in Glasgow, United Kingdom I have had works performed by the Scottish Chamber Orchestra, BBC Scottish Symphony Orchestra, Capella String Quartet, James Grant, Chris Thomson to name a few.

My arrangements have been heard on BBC televison and at the Celtic Connections with Rachel Sermanni, and Karen Matheson, of Capercaillie. Her arrangements have been recorded by ClockworkSessions
