Pianist and Accompanist

As a pianist, I have a wealth of experience as a performer in recitals with other instrumentalists and singers, as well as larger chamber music works. From 2010 - 2015 I was the vocal coach and repetiteur for Cape Town Opera.

In Scotland I have worked with National Youth Choir of Scotland (NYCOS) for auditions as well as rehearsals and concerts. Other choirs include the Bearsden Choir, and Paisley Opera. I have also accompanied musicians for ABRSM exams as well as orchestral auditions and recording tracks for Clockwork Sessions.

Should you need a pianist, feel free to get in touch via my Contact page.
