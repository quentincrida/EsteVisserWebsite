Pianist and Accompanist

As a pianist, Esté has a wealth of experience as a performer in recitals with other instrumentalists and singers, as well as larger chamber music works.She has a wealth of experience working in opera and with choirs. From 2010 until 2015, she was the repetiteur for Cape Town Opera, and has accompanied the National Youth Choir of Scotland (NYCOS) for auditions as well as rehearsals and concerts. Other choirs include the Bearsden Choir
