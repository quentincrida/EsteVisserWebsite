Arranger and Orchestrator

As an arranger, I can set your audio tracks to sheetmusic, or enlarge orchestrations. I do all types of arranging from work for bands to perform live, or record with a live full orchestra.

I can make arrangements for:

  • String Quartet
  • String Trio
  • String Orchestra
  • Chamber Orchestra
  • Full Symphonic OrchestraS
  • Folk Bands
  • Rock Bands

Please go to my Contact page if you would like to start a conversation about your requirements or get a quote. Esté has had works performed by the Scottish Chamber Orchestra, BBC Scottish Symphony Orchestra, Capella String Quartet, James Grant, Chris Thomson to name a few.

My arrangements have been heard on BBC televison and at the Celtic Connections with Rachel Sermanni, and Karen Matheson, of Capercaillie. Her arrangements have been recorded by ClockworkSessions
